Antalya Airport to Boğazkent — what to expect

Boğazkent is located approximately 50 km east of Antalya Airport along the D400 highway, situated between the resort zones of Belek and Side at the mouth of the Köprü River (Eurymedon). The drive takes around 40–50 minutes.

Your chauffeur meets you inside arrivals with a personalised name sign. The transfer is entirely private — no shared routes, no extra stops — and goes directly to your hotel or resort entrance.

Boğazkent is home to several large all-inclusive resort complexes along a scenic stretch of coastline. It is often confused with Belek or Side but has its own hotels and resort village identity.
