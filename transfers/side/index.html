Antalya Airport to Side — what to expect

Side lies 65 km east of Antalya Airport along the D400 coastal highway. The drive takes approximately 55–65 minutes through the resort corridor of the Turkish Riviera, arriving at one of Turkey's most historically rich destinations.

Your chauffeur meets you inside the arrivals hall after baggage reclaim, holding a personalised name sign. Whether your hotel is in the Side old town peninsula, the Sorgun forest resort strip or the Kumköy beach area, your driver knows every route.

Side is famous for its well-preserved Roman ruins, the Temple of Apollo overlooking the sea, and a long sandy coastline flanked by five-star resorts. It combines ancient history with modern beach resort comfort — a journey worth starting right.
